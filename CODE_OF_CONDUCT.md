# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants in this project are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Be inclusive across background, identity, and experience.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in:

- Project repositories and issues
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Discussions and project-managed communication channels

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.
They may edit, remove, or reject contributions and interactions that violate it.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the maintainers through repository owner contact channels.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with confidentiality where possible.

## Attribution

This policy is adapted from common open-source community standards, including the Contributor Covenant.
